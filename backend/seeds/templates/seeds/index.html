Welcome to SoundSeeker, a tool for organizing and juxtaposing musical ideas in the form of short audio recordings. Watch a demo.

There are 3 tiers of organization:

  • Suite - the highest level, represents an overall concept, such as for an album. Suites contain blobs.

  • Blob - a mid-scale idea, such as a song. Blobs contain audioclips.

  • Audioclip - the most granular form of musical idea, like a single musical phrase, bass line, rhythm, sound.

You can associate an audioclip with different blobs, and a blob with different suites. One small idea (audioclip) might be included in multiple songs (blobs). Mix and match however you find useful for your compositional process!
